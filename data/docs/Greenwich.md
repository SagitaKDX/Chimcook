# Greenwich Vietnam 2026 Admissions and Academic Guide

Greenwich Vietnam is an international alliance between the **University of Greenwich (UK)** and **FPT University (Vietnam)**, established in 2009. The program delivers original UK undergraduate curricula entirely in Vietnam.

## 1. Academic Curriculum & Pathways

### 1.1 Learning Pathways
* **Pathway 1 (Direct Entry):** For students with **IELTS 6.0+** or **TOEFL iBT 60+**. These students enter directly into the 3-year major program.
* **Pathway 2 (Preparation Phase):** Students without sufficient English proficiency must complete the **Global Personal Competency (GPC)** program (2–12 months) before starting their specialization.

### 1.2 Global Personal Competency (GPC) Program
The GPC program equips students with 10 core skill sets, including intensive English training across six levels (Level 0 to Level 5).
* **Placement Rules:**
    * **IELTS 4.5:** Default placement into Level 3.
    * **IELTS 5.0:** Default placement into Level 4.
    * **IELTS 5.5:** Default placement into Level 5.
* **English Requirement:** Students must pass Level 5 to advance to their major.

### 1.3 Major Specializations
* **Information Technology:** Includes a specialized track in **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**.
* **Graphic & Digital Design**.
* **Multimedia Communications**.
* **Business Management:** Sub-specializations include Marketing, Events, Communications, International Business, and Logistics & Supply Chain Management.

## 2. Admissions Requirements

### 2.1 Standard Admission Methods
Satisfy one of the following criteria:
* **High School Transcript:** Total score of **21+** in a 3-subject combination (at least one must be Math or Literature).
* **High School Graduation Exam 2026:** Total score of **20+** in any 3-subject combination.
* **Competency Assessments:**
    * **HSA (Hanoi):** 70+.
    * **APT (HCM):** 700+.
    * **SAT:** 800+.
* **International Diplomas:** A-Level, IB, or equivalent international high school diplomas.

### 2.2 Special Tech Requirements
For **IT and AI** majors, students must have a grade of **7.0+** in one of three subjects: Math, Physics, or Chemistry during Grade 11 or Grade 12.

## 3. Tuition Fees & Scholarships

### 3.1 Tuition Fee Structure (Approximate)
| Component | Duration | Fee (VND) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Admission Fee | One-time | 4,600,000 |
| GPC English Level | ~2 Months/Level | 14,800,000 |
| Major Semester | 4 Months/Semester | 50,000,000 |

* **Total 4-Year Tuition (Estimated):** 538,800,000 VND.
* **Refund Policy:** Tuition is non-refundable except in cases where the student fails the High School Graduation Exam.

### 3.2 Scholarship Opportunities
The 2026 scholarship fund totals **250 Billion VND**.
* **Green Talent Scholarship:** 20% to 100% tuition coverage.
* **Criteria:** Based on interview performance and academic metrics (e.g., GPA 9.0+ or IELTS 8.0+).

## 4. Registration & Enrollment Steps

### Online Registration
1.  **Preparation:** Ready scanned ID/Citizen Identity Card (CCCD), birth certificate, and academic transcripts.
2.  **Payment:** Complete the admission fee via bank transfer to the school's account.
3.  **Confirmation:** The school issues a provisional student ID and verifies the application within 2–4 weeks.

### Required Documents (Post-Graduation)
* Certified copies of ID/Citizen Identity Card (CCCD) and Birth Certificate.
* 2 photos (3x4) for the student card.
* IELTS certificate (if applicable).
* High school graduation documents (added after graduation).
