# Greenwich Vietnam - 2026 Admissions and Academic Information

## 1. General Introduction

### 1.1 The First "Super-University" in the UK
* **Merger Event:** In 2026, the University of Greenwich will officially merge with the University of Kent to form a new, large-scale university model, becoming the first "Super-university" in the United Kingdom.
* **Benefits:** This advancement combines the strengths in research, teaching, innovation, and the global partner networks of both institutions.
* **Privileges for Vietnamese Students:** This opens up outstanding advantages in education quality, academic opportunities, exchange programs, and research, while significantly increasing the brand value and prestige of the degree.

### 1.2 University of Greenwich (UK)
* **History:** Established in 1890, it is a prestigious public university in the UK.
* **Notable Achievements:**
    * Achieved a Gold rating (TEF 2023) for outstanding teaching quality.
    * Ranked among the Top 86 universities with the greatest global impact (THE Impact Rankings 2023).
    * Voted by students as the #1 best university in London in 2023 (StudentCrowd).

### 1.3 Greenwich Vietnam
* **History:** Implemented since 2009 through a partnership between the University of Greenwich (UK) and FPT University (Vietnam).
* **Campus Network:** Currently operates 4 campuses nationwide: Hanoi, Da Nang, Ho Chi Minh City, and Can Tho.

## 2. Why Choose Greenwich Vietnam?
* The degree is awarded by the University of Greenwich and is globally recognized.
* The curriculum is 100% originally transferred, utilizing learning materials directly from the UK.
* A standard 3-year study period following the UK higher education system.
* Small class sizes, optimally ranging from 25 to 30 students per class.
* An Academic Advisor (mentor) system to accompany and closely monitor academic progress.
* Integration of the Global Personal Competency (GPC) development program.
* Practical learning at enterprises during the internship phase (OJT - On the Job Training).
* Opportunities to participate in transfer and student exchange programs among the 4 campuses in Vietnam, the UK, and Malaysia.
* 99.3% of graduates secure internationally-oriented employment immediately upon graduation.
* Exclusive privilege to join a global network of over 300,000 alumni.

## 3. Academic Majors (3-Year Bachelor's Programs)

### 3.1 Technology Block (Accredited by the British Computer Society - BCS)
* **Information Technology:** Specialization options in Network Systems or Information Systems.
* **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** Focuses on exploring and developing AI systems, applications, machine learning, and deep learning.

### 3.2 Design & Media Block
* **Graphic & Digital Design:** Balances artistic creative thinking with applied technology (including branding, UI/UX design, and motion graphics).
* **Multimedia Communications:** Provides comprehensive training in digital content production, art direction, journalism, brand strategy, PR, and social media communications.

### 3.3 Business Administration Block (Accredited by the Chartered Management Institute - CMI)
* **Marketing Management:** Ranked Top 4 in London for Marketing (According to the Complete University Guide 2026).
* **Communications Management:** Specializes in communication strategy planning and reputation management.
* **Event Management:** Comprehensive training in the design, finance, marketing, and evaluation of events of all scales.
* **International Business:** Focuses on international business strategies, commerce, and multinational organizational management.
* **Logistics and Supply Chain Management:** Management of processes, distribution, procurement, and applied logistics in retail, manufacturing, and fashion sectors.

## 4. Academic Roadmap

* **Direct Entry to Major (3 Years):** Requires candidates to have an IELTS 6.0+ or TOEFL iBT 60+ certificate (or equivalent).
* **Preparatory English & Skills - GPC Program (2 - 12 Months):** Designed for students who do not yet meet the English entry requirements. The Global Personal Competency (GPC) program equips students with 10 comprehensive skill sets, including: English, critical thinking, leadership, creativity, problem-solving, etc.

## 5. 2026 Admissions Information

### 5.1 Quota and Eligibility
* **National Admission Quota:** 1,800 students.
* **Eligibility:** Students who have graduated from High School or hold an equivalent qualification.

### 5.2 Admission Methods
Candidates can apply for admission through one of the following methods:
1. **High School Transcript (Academic Records):** A total score of **21+ points** from any 3-subject combination (The combination must include either Math or Literature).
2. **2026 High School Graduation Exam Results:** A total exam score of **20+ points** from any 3 subjects.
3. **Competency Assessment Exam Scores:**
   * HSA (Hanoi National University) score ≥ 70 points.
   * APT (Vietnam National University, Ho Chi Minh City) score ≥ 700 points.
   * SAT score ≥ 800 points.
4. **International Affiliate Programs:** Accepts High School diplomas from other countries, A-level certificates, or the International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma.

### 5.3 Scholarship Policies
* **2026 Scholarship Fund:** Total value of up to **250 Billion VND** for incoming freshmen.
* **Special Scholarships:** Greenwich Vietnam continues to sponsor full scholarships (100%) for finalists of the "Road to Mount Olympia" program.
