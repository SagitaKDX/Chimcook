# Greenwich Vietnam - 2026 Admissions and Academic Information

## 1. General Introduction

### 1.1 The First Super-University in the U K
* **The Big Change:** In 2026, the University of Greenwich will join together with the University of Kent. They will become the very first Super-University in the United Kingdom. 
* **The Benefits:** This gives students better classes, more research options, and an internationally recognized college degree.

### 1.2 University of Greenwich in the U K
* **History:** It started in 1890. It is a very famous public university in the U K. 
* **Achievements:** It won a Gold award for great teaching in 2023, and students voted it as the number one best university in London.

### 1.3 Greenwich Vietnam
* **History:** It started in 2009. It is a partnership between the University of Greenwich in the U K, and F P T University in Vietnam. 
* **Locations:** It has four campuses in Vietnam: Ha-noy, Da-nang, Ho Chi Min City, and Kun-tho.

## 2. Why Choose Greenwich Vietnam?
* You get a degree from the University of Greenwich that is famous around the world.
* You read exactly the same books used in the U K.
* You finish your bachelor's degree in exactly 3 years.
* The classes are small, usually about 25 to 30 students.
* You get a personal mentor to help you study.
* You get to do real internships at companies.
* 99 percent of graduating students get a job right away.

## 3. Study Programs (3-Year Bachelor's Degree)

### 3.1 Technology
* **Information Technology:** You can study Network Systems or Information Systems.
* **Artificial Intelligence:** Also known as A I. You learn about machine learning and deep learning.

### 3.2 Design and Media
* **Graphic and Digital Design:** You learn brand design, U I, U X, and video graphics.
* **Media and Communications:** You learn digital content, journalism, P R, and public relations.

### 3.3 Business Management
* **Marketing Management:** It is ranked as a top 4 program in London.
* **Communications Management:** You learn how to manage a company brand.
* **Event Management:** You learn how to plan and run big events.
* **International Business:** You learn how to manage companies around the world.
* **Logistics:** You learn how to manage supply chains and product distribution.

## 4. How to Study

* **Direct Entry:** You study for 3 years. You need an IELTS score of 6 point 0.
* **English Preparation Program:** If your English is not good enough yet, you can study English and important life skills for 2 to 12 months first before you start your real classes.

## 5. 2026 Admissions

### 5.1 Number of Students
* They will accept 1,800 students this year. You just need to graduate from High School.

### 5.2 How to Apply
You can apply in four easy ways:
1. Having a high school transcript score of 21 points or higher.
2. Having a national high school exam score of 20 points or higher.
3. Taking a special university test like the S A T, and scoring 800 points or more.
4. Having an international high school diploma.

### 5.3 Scholarships
* In 2026, they are giving away up to 250 billion Vietnam Dong in scholarships. 
* Very smart students can even get a 100 percent full scholarship if they win the Road to Mount Olympia competition.
