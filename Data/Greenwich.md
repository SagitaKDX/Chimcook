# Greenwich Vietnam 2026 Admissions and Academic Guide

[cite_start]Greenwich Vietnam is an international alliance between the **University of Greenwich (UK)** and **FPT University (Vietnam)**, established in 2009[cite: 1447]. [cite_start]The program delivers original UK undergraduate curricula entirely in Vietnam[cite: 1447, 1663].

## 1. Academic Curriculum & Pathways

### 1.1 Learning Pathways
* **Pathway 1 (Direct Entry):** For students with **IELTS 6.0+** or **TOEFL iBT 60+**. [cite_start]These students enter directly into the 3-year major program[cite: 1673, 3036].
* **Pathway 2 (Preparation Phase):** Students without sufficient English proficiency must complete the **Global Personal Competency (GPC)** program (2–12 months) before starting their specialization[cite: 1674, 3111].

### 1.2 Global Personal Competency (GPC) Program
The GPC program equips students with 10 core skill sets, including intensive English training across six levels (Level 0 to Level 5)[cite: 1531, 3031].
* **Placement Rules:**
    * **IELTS 4.5:** Default placement into Level 3[cite: 3037].
    * [cite_start]**IELTS 5.0:** Default placement into Level 4[cite: 3037].
    * [cite_start]**IELTS 5.5:** Default placement into Level 5[cite: 3038].
* **English Requirement:** Students must pass Level 5 to advance to their major[cite: 1675].

### 1.3 Major Specializations
* [cite_start]**Information Technology:** Includes a specialized track in **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**[cite: 1548, 1557].
* [cite_start]**Graphic & Digital Design**[cite: 1568].
* **Multimedia Communications**[cite: 1578].
* [cite_start]**Business Management:** Sub-specializations include Marketing, Events, Communications, International Business, and Logistics & Supply Chain Management[cite: 1588, 1614, 1627, 1636, 1651].

## 2. Admissions Requirements

### 2.1 Standard Admission Methods
[cite_start]Thỏa mãn one of the following criteria[cite: 1667, 3051]:
* **High School Transcript:** Total score of **21+** in a 3-subject combination (at least one must be Math or Literature)[cite: 1667, 3051].
* [cite_start]**High School Graduation Exam 2026:** Total score of **20+** in any 3-subject combination[cite: 1669].
* **Competency Assessments:**
    * [cite_start]**HSA (Hanoi):** 70+[cite: 1670].
    * **APT (HCM):** 700+[cite: 1671].
    * [cite_start]**SAT:** 800+[cite: 1671].
* [cite_start]**International Diplomas:** A-Level, IB, or equivalent international high school diplomas[cite: 1671, 3122].

### 2.2 Special Tech Requirements
[cite_start]For **IT and AI** majors, students must have a grade of **7.0+** in one of three subjects: Math, Physics, or Chemistry during Grade 11 or Grade 12[cite: 1671, 3054].

| Admission Fee | One-time | [cite_start]4,600,000 [cite: 3049] |

* [cite_start]**Refund Policy:** Tuition is non-refundable except in cases where the student fails the High School Graduation Exam[cite: 3047, 3108].

### 3.2 Scholarship Opportunities
[cite_start]The 2026 scholarship fund totals **250 Billion VND**[cite: 1677].
* **Green Talent Scholarship:** 20% to 100% tuition coverage[cite: 3057].
* [cite_start]**Criteria:** Based on interview performance and academic metrics (e.g., GPA 9.0+ or IELTS 8.0+)[cite: 3057].

## 4. Registration & Enrollment Steps

### Online Registration
1.  [cite_start]**Preparation:** Ready scanned ID/CCCD, birth certificate, and academic transcripts[cite: 3102].
2.  [cite_start]**Payment:** Complete the admission fee via bank transfer to the school's account[cite: 3043].
3.  [cite_start]**Confirmation:** The school issues a provisional student ID and verifies the application within 2–4 weeks[cite: 3046, 3107].

### Required Documents (Post-Graduation)
* Certified copies of ID/CCCD and Birth Certificate[cite: 3102].
* 2 photos (3x4) for the student card.
* IELTS certificate (if applicable)[cite: 3102].
* [cite_start]High school graduation documents (added after graduation)[cite: 3102].
