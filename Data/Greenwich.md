# Greenwich Vietnam 2026 Admissions and Academic Guide
Greenwich Vietnam is an international partnership between the University of Greenwich in the U K, and F P T University in Vietnam. It was established in 2009. The program delivers original U K undergraduate classes entirely in Vietnam.

## 1. Academic Curriculum and Pathways

### 1.1 Learning Pathways
* **Direct Entry:** For students with an IELTS score of 6 point 0 or higher. These students enter directly into the 3-year major program.
* **Preparation Phase:** Students without enough English skills must complete the Global Personal Competency program for 2 to 12 months before starting their real classes.

### 1.2 Global Personal Competency Program
This program teaches students 10 core skill sets, including intensive English training across six levels.
* **Placement Rules:**
    * **IELTS 4 point 5:** You start at Level 3.
    * **IELTS 5 point 0:** You start at Level 4.
    * **IELTS 5 point 5:** You start at Level 5.
* **English Requirement:** Students must pass Level 5 to advance to their main college major.

### 1.3 Major Specializations
* **Information Technology:** Includes a specialized track in Artificial Intelligence, also known as A I.
* **Graphic and Digital Design.**
* **Multimedia Communications.**
* **Business Management:** You can specialize in Marketing, Events, Communications, International Business, or Logistics.

## 2. Admissions Requirements

### 2.1 Standard Admission Methods
You must meet one of the following requirements:
* **High School Transcript:** A total score of 21 points or higher in a 3-subject combination. At least one subject must be Math or Literature.
* **High School Graduation Exam 2026:** A total score of 20 points or higher in any 3-subject combination.
* **Competency Assessments:**
    * H S A test in Ha-noy: 70 points or higher.
    * A P T test in Ho Chi Min City: 700 points or higher.
    * S A T test: 800 points or higher.
* **International Diplomas:** A-Level, I B, or equivalent international high school diplomas.

### 2.2 Special Tech Requirements
For I T and A I majors, students must have a grade of 7 point 0 or higher in either Math, Physics, or Chemistry during Grade 11 or Grade 12.

## 3. Tuition Fees and Scholarships

### 3.1 Tuition Fee Structure
* **Admission Fee:** This is a one-time fee of 4 million, 600 thousand Vietnam Dong.
* **English Level Fee:** Each english level takes about 2 months and costs 14 million, 800 thousand Vietnam Dong.
* **Major Semester Fee:** Each main college semester takes 4 months and costs 50 million Vietnam Dong.
* **Total 4-Year Tuition:** The estimated total cost is 538 million, 800 thousand Vietnam Dong.
* **Refund Policy:** Tuition is non-refundable, except if the student fails their High School Graduation Exam.

### 3.2 Scholarship Opportunities
The 2026 scholarship fund has a total of 250 Billion Vietnam Dong.
* **Green Talent Scholarship:** This covers 20 percent to 100 percent of your tuition.
* **Criteria:** It is based on an interview and your grades. For example, having a G P A of 9 point 0, or an IELTS score of 8 point 0.

## 4. Registration and Enrollment Steps

### Online Registration
1. **Preparation:** Have your scanned I D card, birth certificate, and high school transcripts ready.
2. **Payment:** Pay the admission fee through a bank transfer to the school's account.
3. **Confirmation:** The school will give you a temporary student I D, and verify your application within 2 to 4 weeks.

### Required Documents
* Certified copies of your I D card and Birth Certificate.
* 2 photos for your student card.
* Your IELTS certificate if you have one.
* Your high school graduation documents.
